Tajweed Course – Learn to Recite the Quran Correctly

Tajweed means "to beautify" and refers to the rules of proper pronunciation while reciting the Quran. In this course, you’ll learn how to pronounce each letter from its correct place (Makharij), apply rules like Ikhfa, Idghaam, and Qalqalah, and recite the Quran the way it was revealed. This is a separate course focused purely on accurate and beautiful recitation.
