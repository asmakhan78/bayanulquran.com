Our Courses

Courses We Offer

We offer a variety of Quran and Islamic studies.

Quran Reading (Nazra) Course

Who it's for: Beginners who want to learn how to read the Quran fluently.

What it covers:

  • Arabic alphabet and pronunciation
  • Joining letters and basic word reading
  • Fluency without applying Tajweed rules

Quran Memorization (Hifz) Course

Who it's for: Students who want to memorize the Quran with proper guidance.

What it covers:

  • Memorization techniques
  • Daily revision plans
  • Teacher-led correction

Quran with Translation and Tafsir

Who it's for: Intermediate learners who want to understand the meaning of the Quran.

What it covers:

  • Word-for-word translation
  • Short Tafsir (explanation) of verses
  • Practical lessons from the Quran

About Us

We are an online platform focused on teaching the Quran with proper Tajweed. Our experienced female instructors guide students—especially children—through live,
interactive sessions. Many learners have been with us for over a year, growing steadily in their recitation and understanding.
